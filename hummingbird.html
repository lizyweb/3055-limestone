Follow Us On

Discover the enchanting allure of Limestone’s farmhouse community, Hummingbird in Coimbatore, where 6 acres of picturesque land host 19 charming homes, weaving a tapestry of comfort and tranquillity. Nestled amidst verdant surroundings, our luxury farmhouse beckons you to unwind and reconnect with nature's rhythm. Here, luxury extends far beyond the confines of four walls, embracing you in the vast expanse of the outdoors. Our individual villas offer the perfect retreat to escape the hustle and bustle of daily life. Whether you're exploring the countryside, savouring moments with loved ones, or simply immersing yourself in the warmth of family, every experience at Limestone is a timeless escapade, where memories are etched against the backdrop of serene landscapes and cherished bonds.

At Hummingbird, the enchanting allure extends to the thoughtful design of our community, which is divided into two distinct areas: North Facing Villas and South Facing Villas. This arrangement ensures that each residence enjoys a unique perspective and ambiance, tailored to enhance the living experience. In the North Facing Villas, residents can bask in the gentle morning sunlight, perfect for early risers who appreciate starting their day with the soft glow of dawn. These independent villas offer a serene and tranquil environment, ideal for those who seek a peaceful retreat amidst the verdant surroundings. Conversely, the South Facing Villas are bathed in the warm afternoon sun, creating a cozy and inviting atmosphere throughout the day. These homes are well-suited for those who enjoy spending their afternoons and evenings in the comfort of their outdoor spaces, making the most of the beautiful landscapes that surround them.

Whether you choose a North Facing Villa or a South Facing Villa, Hummingbird promises an experience where luxury and nature intertwine seamlessly, offering a timeless escapade filled with cherished memories and tranquil moments.

Drip Irrigation

Drip irrigation is a water-efficient method that delivers small, controlled amounts of water directly to plant roots, promoting healthy growth and minimizing waste.

Drip irrigation efficiently delivers water to plants' root zones through a system of tubing and emitters, reducing water waste and promoting healthier plant growth. It conserves water by minimizing evaporation and runoff, making it particularly suitable for arid regions or areas with limited water availability.

Vermicompost Pit

In our commitment to sustainable practices, we proudly employ a vermicompost pit, refraining entirely from the use of chemicals and opting solely for naturally acquired organic fertilizer.

This method not only ensures the health and vitality of our soil but also underscores our dedication to environmental stewardship. Through the collaborative efforts of earthworms and organic waste, we cultivate nutrient-rich compost, enriching the land and fostering a thriving ecosystem within our gardens.
