Who We Are

Welcome to Limestone, where we craft more than just homes; we create serene escapes amidst nature's embrace. As a close-knit team of passionate individuals, we are dedicated to transforming raw land into exclusive luxury villas within gated communities that resonate with tranquillity and sophistication. Led by our visionary Managing Director, who possesses a deep-rooted connection to the land, Limestone stands as a testament to our commitment to harmonizing urban living with nature's serenity.

Each community we create offers a sanctuary from the hustle and bustle of everyday life, inviting residents to unwind and reconnect with the essence of peaceful living. At Limestone, we believe in crafting more than just residences; we curate lifestyles. Our maiden project, Hummingbird, featuring an exclusive independent house in Coimbatore and individual villas is just the beginning of our journey toward redefining contemporary living spaces. Nestled in the heart of coconut farms in Coimbatore, Hummingbird is a pioneering real estate venture that embodies our vision of modern living in Premium villas. With an unwavering dedication to quality craftsmanship and sustainable development practices, we strive to set new benchmarks in the real estate industry.

Join us on our quest to build not just homes, but havens where every moment is an opportunity to embrace the peacefulness of nature and find solace amidst the tranquil greenery. Discover your oasis with Limestone, where luxury meets nature in perfect harmony! Explore the best luxury villas in Coimbatore we offer, designed to provide an unparalleled living experience.

Our Vision

Our current way of life has left us longing for a balance between the convenience of modern living with the best villas and the tranquillity of natural surroundings. Our aim with our first project, Hummingbird, is to preserve the beauty and functionality of the coconut groves by carefully designing our homes around them. This makes it the best area to buy a villa in Coimbatore, ensuring minimal disturbance or harm. By doing so, we hope to create an escape from the hustle and bustle of cities, residents can immerse themselves in a peaceful and sustainable living environment. These premium villas in Coimbatore will offer the perfect blend of modern amenities and a serene setting. allowing individuals to embrace a more natural lifestyle.
